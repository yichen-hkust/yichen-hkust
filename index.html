About me

I am an assistant professor at departement of Industry Engineering and Decision Analytics, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) since 2022. Before that, I obtained my PhD degree from department of IEMS, Northwestern University and bachelor degree from department of statistics, University of Science and Technology of China. My research interests include data-driven decision-making and empirical operations management, with applications in healthcare. My primary research interest is designing data-driven algorithms to solve real problems in operations research. I am also interested in empirical research related to service system operations.
