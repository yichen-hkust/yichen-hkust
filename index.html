About me

I am an assistant professor at departement of Industry Engineering and Decision Analytics, Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) since 2022. My research interests include data-driven decision-making and empirical operations management, with applications in healthcare. Before joining HKUST, I obtained my PhD degree from department of IEMS, Northwestern University and bachelor degree from department of statistics, University of Science and Technology of China.
